Helpful Information

Questions You May Have

We're here to guide you through every step of your child's developmental journey.

How do we start therapy?

Simply book a free consultation. We'll listen to your concerns, answer your questions, and find the best path forward for your child.

Do you support my insurance?

Yes, we partner with major providers like Aetna, Cigna, and BlueCross. Our team handles all the details so you can focus on your child.

Is online therapy effective?

Absolutely. Our engaging, play-centered teletherapy is designed to be just as effective as in-person visits, but much more convenient for your family.
